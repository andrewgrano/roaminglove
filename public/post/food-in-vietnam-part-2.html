Food In Vietnam: Part 2

By Sigournee Grano on September 13, 2016

  • Asia
  • Vietnam
  • Food

My absolute favorite meal in Vietnam was at Chicken Street. They sell chicken thighs, wings and breasts on sticks. They are seasoned and grilled right in front of you. The sides were the best part. They serve bánh mì coated in honey and grilled to perfection. Better yet, they serve potatoes on a stick.
