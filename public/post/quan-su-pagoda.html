Quan Su Pagoda

April 07, 2016

  • Asia
  • Vietnam
  • Stories

Andrew and I woke up in the morning, earlier than planned. We researched something local to do and we found a Pagoda nearby. It wasn’t too far from our apartment so we walked there.

This place was such a surprise! It is a Buddhist Temple in the middle of Hanoi. It was so calming and peaceful.  I have never heard Monks chant before but it was very soothing. There were a lot of people around so we made sure to be respectful. You cannot go into the temple unless you remove your shoes. You cannot even photograph too close to the temple before someone yells at you to take off your shoes so remember that if you decide to come here.
