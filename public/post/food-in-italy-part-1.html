Food In Italy: Part 1

By Sigournee Grano on September 09, 2016

  • Europe
  • Italy
  • Food

We chose Italy as our first European destination because of the food. We both love pizza and pasta. I personally love Parmesan cheese. I knew I had to visit Italy. We ate a lot of pizza. Also, cornettos. We ate a lot of those. In fact, I think I ate a cornetto everyday for breakfast. Fine, I ate 2 cornettos everyday for breakfast.
