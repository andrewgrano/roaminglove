Bia Hoi

April 09, 2016

  • Asia
  • Vietnam
  • Food

My friend Natalie took us to the local Bia Hoi for dinner and drinks. The Bia Hoi specializes in daily beer that costs about 10 cents a glass. The beer is made daily and has a shelf life of 24 hours. It was such an authentic Vietnamese experience! We also ate very well for quite inexpensive.
