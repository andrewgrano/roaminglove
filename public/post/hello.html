Hello!

By Sigournee Grano on April 02, 2016

Hello. My name is Sigournee and I have decided to document my travels. Andrew is my fiance and you will see a lot of him on this blog too! Andrew and I have bonded over our love of music, pizza and adventure. We reside in Los Angeles. Tomorrow we embark on our biggest trip yet; we are headed to Vietnam & Cambodia. We love to travel together! In fact, we have traveled with each other many times before!

Our first trip together was to San Francisco in 2010. As two 20 year olds from San Diego, trekking to San Francisco was a big deal. My cousin Zach and his then girlfriend (now wife) Lindsay, let us stay at there apartment. It was in Hayes Valley so we ate so much good food that weekend. I recommend Patxi’s Pizza whenever anyone is visiting San Francisco. It’s a must! We also managed to unknowingly arrive in the city as Gay Pride was starting. We had no idea it was even taking place that weekend!

We traveled to Portland in 2014. We ate a lot of food. Andrew drank a lot of beer. It was a great time, mostly because you can play slot machines in bars! My best friend Audra lives there so we stayed with her.

Our most memorable trip happened May 2015. We backpacked Italy for 2 weeks. We went to Rome, Florence, Bologna, Venice, & Cinque Terre. Everything we ate was delicious. Italy is a beautiful country and I hope to go back one day! Trastevere in Rome was Andrew and I’s absolute favorite part of the city. We had a long layover in Dusseldorf, Germany on the way back to the USA. Dusseldorf is a very nice city. So clean!

I can’t forget Alaska. We spent a week there in July 2015. My family and I stayed in Anchorage and we absolutely loved it. Alaska is so naturally stunning. Also, the sun didn’t really set so that was pretty crazy. I would definitely go back to Alaska one day!

I will try my best to keep up with blogging our time in Southeast Asia. We are VERY excited for this trip. We are traveling and staying with my friend Natalie, who I met the first week of college. I will be posting here as we go along! Thank you for reading.
