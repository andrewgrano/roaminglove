By The Banyan Tree

By Shareen Cassy Karunaratne on December 08, 2016

  • Middle East
  • United Arab Emirates

Although I love to travel around the world, sometimes it’s always great to explore the different destinations within the United Arab Emirates. Where better to start than at Banyan Tree Beach in Ras al-Khaimah.

My best friend and I decided to join the parents for a long weekend getaway. Heading out at 10 a.m. we reached Al Hamra Village at 12 p.m. and were told that we had to take a boat ride from the car park to the hotel; how exciting!  Once we reached the hotel and checked ourselves in, we were escorted to our villa. From that point on, it was a relaxing weekend of swimming, eating, sipping wine and morning walks on the beach.
