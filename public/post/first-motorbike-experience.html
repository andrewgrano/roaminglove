First motorbike experience

April 09, 2016

Almost everyone in Hanoi, Vietnam rides a motorbike. There are even motorbike taxis. After watching the locals for a day, we had to experience it for ourselves.

It was such a rush of adrenaline. It is also surprisingly safe! I can’t wait to do it again one day.

We stopped at a beautiful cathedral on the way too! I would highly recommend a motorbike experience in Hanoi!
