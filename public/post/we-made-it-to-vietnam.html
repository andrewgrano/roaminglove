We made it to Vietnam

April 04, 2016

We safely arrived in Hanoi, Vietnam last night around 10 pm local time. We were greeted at the airport by my best friend and bridesmaid, Natalie! She is a teacher in Hanoi for the next 2 years! 

  

The plane ride wasn’t actually that bad! It was 12 hours from LAX to Seoul, South Korea. We had a short layover there and then we flew another 4 hours to Hanoi. We slept that entire 4 hours, haha. Before landing in Seoul, we flew over these amazing islands! It was also fun to receive a receipt completely in Korean. 

  
  
It is 6:55 am here now. I am looking forward to exploring Hanoi today!
