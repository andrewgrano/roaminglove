Traveling with my best friend

April 12, 2016

I met Natalie the first week of my freshman year of college. We lived across the hall from each other. That was 8 years ago. Today, we live on opposite sides of the world but she is still one of my best friends. In fact, she is a bridesmaid in my wedding this summer!

Natalie is currently a teacher in Hanoi, Vietnam. Her apartment is located a few blocks from Hanoi’s old quarter. Natalie also traveled to Cambodia with me. I am so happy to have these new memories with my best friend.

Our significant other’s tease us that we talk the same. We do! Natalie and I have all the same interests. Sometimes when we talk to each other, people don’t know what we are saying. Valley girls for life. 

I love you, Natalie!
