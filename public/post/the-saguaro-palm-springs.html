The Saguaro: Palm Springs

By Sigournee Grano on November 30, 2016

  • California
  • United States
  • Hotels

The Saguaro is a dream hotel. There are colors everywhere. No seriously, every wall, door and even bed frame, is a different color in this quirky hotel. The Saguaro is located on a main street in Palm Springs. You are able to walk across the street to a grocery store and ATM. You can drive down the street to the Downtown area of Palm Springs. We stayed here for 2 nights.

We walked into the hotel and I was blown away with how much effort was put into just the lobby. It is inviting and immediately makes you want to have a drink by the pool. The front desk staff were young, friendly and fast at checking us in. They even let us check in early!

I was so excited to see our room! I knew that every room was a different color so I was excited to see what color we got. Our room was yellow and lime green! Our bathroom was orange! The room has everything you will need for a nice stay; comfy bed, coffee maker, mini fridge, TV, private bathroom and large balcony. The hotel feels like summertime never goes away. We stepped onto our balcony and gazed upon the pool. We immediately changed into our bathing suits and headed straight to the pool. I have seen SO many Instagram photos of the pool at The Saguaro that my mouth dropped when I saw it! It is not only huge but the backdrop is gorgeous mountains that look like a painting. Of course, palm trees didn’t hurt either.

The pool area has 2 hot tubs and a walk up bar that also serves food. We ordered tacos and chips/salsa at the bar and the waitress brought it right to our lounge chair. The food was reasonably priced and delicious. Also, the portions were big! Maybe it was because we visited the hotel during Autumn instead of summertime but it was so relaxing. There is always trendy indie music playing and the pool is always scattered with fun pool floats. Although it was off-season for the hotel, there were still plenty of couples and groups of friends laying around the pool. The water was definitely too cold to swim in but the entire atmosphere is really enjoyable. We explored the hotel grounds and there is lots to do! They have a yoga studio, gym, corn hole, bocce ball and on-site bar and restaurant.

I would highly recommend a visit to this hotel! Even if you cannot stay here, try your best to check out the pool area! It is worth a look. Huge thank you to The Saguaro for hosting us on behalf of Roaming Love. We enjoyed our stay and I don’t think I will ever visit a hotel as colorful as this one!

Take a look at The Saguaro here: thesaguaro.com/palm-springs
