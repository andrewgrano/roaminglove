Food In Vietnam: Part 1

By Sigournee Grano on September 13, 2016

  • Asia
  • Vietnam
  • Food

I am a picky eater. I am allergic to a lot of things, primarily citrus and nuts. I do not eat seafood. When planning my trip to Vietnam, I was petrified about the food. What would I eat? Hanoi has plenty of food options for all types of eaters. You can find almost any cuisine in the city. There are endless cafes that sell not only coffee but sandwiches and pastries. If you are a picky eater and/or have lots of food restrictions, do not fret! Hanoi is still a great travel option. Food is cheap here and beer is even cheaper.
