Torrevieja

By 김경진 on March 02, 2017

  • Europe
  • Spain

I’m a traveler from South Korea (Republic of Korea). I backpacked around Europe & Morocco for 66 days! The place that really stood out to me is Torrevieja in Spain. It is located in the Alicante province. The rays of the pink there are stunning . There is a very beautiful lake that glistens pink during sunset! The lake has white salt too instead of sand! Torrevieja is the most beautiful place I’ve ever been. I have never seen such amazing sunsets. The pretty scenery delighted my eyes! I was lucky to have my 66 day trip end on such a high note in Torrevieja. I want to visit again someday. It was a dream trip and I hope my photos speak for themselves.
